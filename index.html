📍 About Me

I'm Lexi Barry, an Associate Client Manager with expertise in CPG analytics, brand storytelling, and transforming complex data into growth strategies. My work bridges business questions with clear data-driven solutions.

At NielsenIQ, I’ve worked closely with clients across multiple categories and sectors. My experience includes supporting Johnson & Johnson (now Kenvue) in a variety of health and wellness segments — including sun care, self care, hair care, and over-the-counter medicine such as Zyrtec, Benadryl, Sudafed, Tylenol, and Motrin. I developed category insights to guide innovation, pricing strategy, and promotional execution.

Currently, I lead analysis for United Natural Foods Inc. (UNFI), focusing on the grocery sector. My work spans a wide range of departments including fresh, grocery, frozen, dairy, meat, pre-packaged meat, health, beauty, and home. I help brands and retailers within UNFI’s network make data-driven decisions to grow their footprint in natural, organic, and conventional grocery channels.
