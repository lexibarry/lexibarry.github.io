📍 About Me

I'm Lexi Barry, an Associate Client Manager with expertise in CPG analytics, brand storytelling, and transforming complex data into growth strategies. My work bridges business questions with clear data-driven solutions.

At NielsenIQ, I’ve partnered with clients across a wide range of industries and departments. I supported Johnson & Johnson (now Kenvue) on category growth strategy across sun care, self care, hair care, and over-the-counter (OTC) medicine — including brands like Zyrtec, Benadryl, Sudafed, Tylenol, and Motrin. I helped shape pricing, promo strategy, and innovation through deep data insights.

Currently, I lead insights for United Natural Foods Inc. (UNFI), one of the largest wholesale grocery distributors. I support brands and retailers across the grocery sector, including fresh, frozen, dairy, grocery, beverages, snacks, meat, pre-packaged meat, health, beauty, personal care, natural, home, and more. I help identify white space opportunities, optimize assortment and promotion, and guide strategic category decisions using large, complex datasets.
