Function Health: Strategic One-Pager

Confidential analysis prepared by Lexi Barry

📋 Executive Summary

Function Health is a wellness diagnostics and data platform focused on proactive health monitoring through comprehensive biomarker testing and personalized recommendations. This analysis outlines opportunities to improve user retention, expand subscription tiers, and increase lifetime value through improved member education, habit loops, and partner integrations.

🔍 Key Insights

  • Members engaging with the app weekly show a 2.8× higher renewal rate.
  • Top churn drivers: delayed lab results and lack of perceived long-term value.
  • Integrating wearable syncs (Oura, Whoop, Apple Health) can reduce cancellations by ~15%.

🚀 Strategic Recommendations

  • Retention Focus: Introduce milestone badges for test frequency and engagement streaks.
  • Partnership Expansion: Collaborate with gym franchises and corporate wellness programs.
  • Upsell Path: Develop a premium “Function+” membership with family accounts and advanced metrics.
