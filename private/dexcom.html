Product Concept: Metabolic Stabilizer™

“Intervene before the spike.” As an active user of the Stelo sensor and my Oura Ring, I’ve lived the gap: seeing the spike after it happens isn’t enough. I propose a forward-looking micro-coach inside Stelo: predicts likely glucose surges and delivers one simple lifestyle nudge (walk 10 minutes, add a protein bite, hydrate) in the moment. This turns passive tracking into real-time decision support for non-insulin-users — positioning Stelo as the daily metabolic companion, not just the tracker.

🎯 User Value

“Tell me what to do *now* so I avoid the crash later.” Shifts from reflection to action for everyday metabolic health.

⚙️ Implementation Path

Phase 1: model short-term glucose slope + meal tagging. Trigger a “Stabilize Now” card when trend threshold reached. Phase 2: layer Oura readiness/recovery data to tailor intensity of suggestion.

📊 Success Metrics

Boost in daily active sessions (2hr post-meal), reduced time-above-target after meals, increased sensor renewal/ retention.
