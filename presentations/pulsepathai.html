Insight

AI Coaching in EdTech and Claims Automation in Health Insurance show both high engagement and retention. These use cases merit increased investment and focused onboarding.

Slide 7 of 10

Recommendations

  • Prioritize AI Coaching and Claims Automation in roadmap planning
  • Improve onboarding in use cases with lower trial retention (e.g., Compliance Assist)
  • Align GTM strategy with high-retention industries (EdTech, Health)

Roadmap Snapshot

  • Next (30–60d): A/B test feature flows
  • Later (60–90d): Scale across verticals
