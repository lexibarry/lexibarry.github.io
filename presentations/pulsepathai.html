Key risks include data integration complexity and regulatory requirements; mitigation strategies in place

Risk Assessment & Mitigation

1 Risk: EHR Integration Challenges

Impact: High | Hospital systems have complex IT environments with legacy EHRs

Mitigation: Pre-built connectors for top 5 EHRs (Epic, Cerner, Meditech); dedicated integration team; 30-day guarantee

2 Risk: Regulatory & Compliance (HIPAA, FDA)

Impact: High | Software changes to clinical workflows may require FDA clearance

Mitigation: Designed as clinical decision support (CDS) to avoid FDA regulation; HITRUST certification; BAA-compliant architecture

3 Risk: Model Accuracy & Adoption

Impact: Medium | Clinicians may distrust AI recommendations or system may underperform

Mitigation: Continuous model retraining; transparent "explainable AI" dashboard; phased rollout with change management support

4 Risk: Competitive Pressure

Impact: Medium | Large EHR vendors may build competing features

Mitigation: Speed to market advantage; superior ML models; EHR partnerships (co-sell agreements with Epic, Cerner)

Source: Risk assessment workshop with clinical advisors and legal counsel 10 of 12
