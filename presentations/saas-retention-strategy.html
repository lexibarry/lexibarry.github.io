⚠️ Risks & Mitigation Strategies

🚨 Risk: Over-Communication Fatigue

Users may feel bombarded with emails and in-app prompts, leading to opt-outs or negative sentiment.

✅ Mitigation Strategy

  • • Cap email frequency at 1 per week max
  • • Use behavioral triggers (not time-based)
  • • A/B test messaging cadence
  • • Provide easy opt-out controls

🚨 Risk: Feature Complexity

If Pro features are genuinely too complex, education alone won't drive adoption and retention.

✅ Mitigation Strategy

  • • Conduct UX (User Experience) audit in parallel
  • • Simplify onboarding flows for top 3 features
  • • Add interactive tooltips and guided tours
  • • Track activation friction points

🚨 Risk: Execution Delays

Engineering or marketing capacity constraints could push timeline and delay impact.

✅ Mitigation Strategy

  • • Secure exec sponsorship and priority
  • • Use existing marketing automation tools
  • • Start with manual CSM outreach (MVP)
  • • Phase rollout to reduce scope risk
